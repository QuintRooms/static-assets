Meet the Lowest Rate Guarantee

We are putting our money where our mouth is. Select hotel rates with this icon are backed by our Lowest Rate Guarantee. Find a lower rate and our partners at QuintRooms will not only match it, but will throw in an extra $10. No questions asked.

The fine print:

Not all room rates are created equal. Please note, to qualify for the Lowest Rate Guarantee the following must apply:

  • Qualified rooms must be at the exact same property, for the exact same room type, exact same dates, exact same number of guests, and the exact same terms (i.e. cancellation policy, deposit policy, etc.)
  • Qualified rooms must be identified within 24 hours of when the original reservation was booked
  • Qualified rooms must be accessible via publicly available websites (meaning there is no membership or login required to access the rate)
  • The hotel rate must have the Lowest Rate Guarantee shield icon designation

Lowest Rate Guarantee does not apply to special group, event, member, or association rate(s).
