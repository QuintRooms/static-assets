Where Are My Festival Passes?
Please note, you will collect your festival passes at our Hotel Experience Package Check In table after you check in. Please be on the lookout for our Tortuga Hotel Experience package check in signage or ask the front desk. Credentials will be checked. Please be sure to bring your ID. The ID must match the name on file for the travel package.
  • Hotel Experience Package Check-In: Friday, April 8th from 8:00 AM to 5:00 PM
  • If you plan on picking up your packet AFTER 5:00pm on Friday, April 8th, please notify us at (512)691-9555 or reservations@quintrooms.com.
  • Any passes NOT picked up on Friday, April 8th will be available at Will Call at the Festival Box Office on Saturday, April 9th from 10:00 AM to 12:00 PM.

TORTUGA ENTRY PROCEDURES 2021- Safety Requirement
TORTUGA MUSIC FESTIVAL IS DEDICATED TO THE HEALTH AND SAFETY OF OUR FANS, STAFF AND ARTISTS.

Prior to entry into the festival, attendees will be required to provide:
A printed copy of a negative COVID-19 test result. The negative COVID-19 printed test result must be obtained within 72 hours of entering Tortuga Music Festival each day you attend and the COVID-19 test result must include your name, date of test, and test result.

Patrons who are fully vaccinated may show proof of full COVID-19 vaccination instead of proof of negative test. Patrons showing proof of vaccine do not need to be tested to enter the festival. A person is considered fully vaccinated 14 days after the second shot of Pfizer/Moderna or single shot of J&J. Guests are encouraged to wear a mask if they’d like to.
